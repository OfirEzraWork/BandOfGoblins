Welcome to the world of Atlas!

Explore the world of Atlas. Plunder the many ruins of the civilizations of the first age. Lead kingdoms to war or civil unrest. Acquire the seven eyes of the seven beasts and bring ruin to all. Explore Massad's devouring desert and uncover ancient secrets of the gods. Or open a bakery and walk in the path of capitalism.

Either way, the world is yours to conquer. What you do is up to you. In Atlas anything is possible.

Adventuring in Atlas

  • Politics

    A civil unrest grows in Lenora as the territories of the Atherian kingdom fight to gain autonomy.

    The Liv'tharel society and the Alliance of the Vokar from the continet Glevritiel are in an arms race. Both eager to find the weapon to win the next war.

    The city states of Massad are engaged in a contient scale game of political conquest to gain wealth and power.

  • Dragons

    The ancient blue dragon Umreth the Arcanist is working on a ritual to become a Dracolich. Leading his Tlincalli army he intends to conquer Massad.

    The rest of the Flight of Tiamat's ancient dragons whereabouts are currently unknown

  • Artifacts

    Many unique artifacts are scattered throughout the world. Powerful magical items with the potential to change world orders or bring destruction and ruin.

    The seven eyes of the seven beasts await.

  • Family

    Do you have a family?

    Think again!

    In Atlas your family either dies horribly, gets kidnapped or is banishing you from their lives due to nature of your adventuring profession.

    Many nights will be spent alone and thinking of what you've lost when you chose to follow the adventurer path!
